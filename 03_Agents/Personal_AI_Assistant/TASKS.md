# TASKS

> 核心工作職責，依優先順序排列。

## Main Jobs（優先順序）
1. **Pipeline & Forecast Tracking**（最優先）: Assist in organizing ad booking data, tracking weekly ad revenue progress against targets, and keeping client account lists up to date.
2. **Key Account Management**（第二優先）: Personally manage or lead the team in interfacing with top-tier brands on the platform and non-merchant advertising revenue including HK government, bank.
3. Cross-Functional Coordination: Act as a liaison between Ad Sales, Product Marketing, Campaign Operations, and IT to track project timelines (e.g., modules, revamp launches, or new ad placements).
4. Expense & Budget Tracking: Manage the department's corporate expense approvals, client entertainment tracking, and vendor payments.

> 3、4 項目前無特定優先順序（使用者表示暫不需要排序）。
